When the Holidays are hard

Grief can hit especially hard during the holidays, when carols, decorations, and traditions remind you of the special times you shared with a loved one you lost or a relationship that ended. It may be especially difficult to pick up the pieces and get through another holiday after suffering a loss. How do you relate when everything around you is festive and joyful and you are feeling so very sad? Read how your church family is here to walk with you this season in the Grapevine.
